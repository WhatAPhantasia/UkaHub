.x* ME AND THE DEMON KING *x.

Ari is a non-descript boy- Though the same couldn't be said for his shadow.
An Okage: Shadow King ukagaka. Interact with Ari and Stan: They can take out your recycling bin, check your emails, give you reminders, and have general banter and the like. After a while, maybe Ari will start opening up to you?

- - IMPORTANT - -
-You can use "Shell Scaling" the SSP menu to change his size.
-Make sure to uncheck Options > Preferences > ghost(2) > Balloon Scaling Linkage so her speech bubbles stay consistent when you scale him!
-You can uninstall him by deleting the ph_bokutomaou folder out of your ssp/ghost/ directory, or by turning on uninstall in his personal menu, then selecting 'Uninstall' in the SSP menu.

-- Quick User Guide --
-Move your mouse back and forth over his head to pet him (you don't have to click)
-Double click his face to punch him (it'll warn you first!)
-Double click his body to open his personal menu
-Right click him to open the SSP menu
-Hit the T key when he's the focus to make him say something
-Hit the R key to make him repeat what he just said
-Hold down CTRL to speed through text
